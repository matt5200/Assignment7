Straight To The Point

“Since 2005, we’ve dedicated ourselves to teaching valuable life skills to youth in our community through the wonderful discipline of archery. “ - GIAP NGUYEN, FOUNDER

Learn from qualified instructors with extensive knolwedge of the most advanced archery equipment and all forms of archery from the traidtional long bow to the modern compound. Our staff decades of experience in the archery industry. We are committed to educating you with any questions that you may have.

Grow your skills in our indoor training facility with 28 shooting (14 - 10 yard lanes and 14 – 20 yard lanes) and 2 private coaching rooms. We also have access to 26 additional 20 yard lanes and an outdoor shooting field.

Join our club and gain access to our excellent network of contacts with the USA Archery (USAA), Washington State Archery Association (WSAA), Washington State Bow Hunters (WSB), National Field Archery Association (NFAA) and many more national and state archery associations.
